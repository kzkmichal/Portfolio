Sponsorships

West Ham

Our partnership with this great club has given us huge exposure worldwide and coincided with one of the most exciting periods in the history of West Ham, as they bid farewell to the historic Boleyn Ground and started their latest chapter at the London Stadium.

Ninjas in Pyjamas

Betway was the first major bookmaker to launch a dedicated eSports portal back in 2015. Our record sponsorship deal with Ninjas in Pyjamas, one of the most renowned professional Counter-Strike teams in the world, gives fans the opportunity to get closer to the players than ever before.

Cheltenham

Cheltenham is the highlight of the National Hunt season and, starting at the 2015 festival, Betway became sponsors of one of the most prestigious races of the calendar, the Betway Queen Mother Champion Chase. The championship race has a history of esteemed winners including the legendary 2016 victor Sprinter Sacre, Special Tiara and modern great Altior.

All-Weather Championships

As part of our continued commitment to British and Irish horse racing, in 2016, we became category sponsors of the All-Weather Championships encompassing the Sprint, Middle Distance and Marathon categories of the Championships, including the Betway Easter Classic on Good Friday.

Grand National

The Grand National Festival at Aintree is one of the biggest and most anticipated fixtures of the racing calendar. As official betting partner of the three-day festival, we are proud to sponsor four Grade One contests: the Betway Bowl, Betway Aintree Hurdle, Betway Mildmay Novices' Chase and Betway Mersey Novices' Hurdle.

World Snooker

Betway became the title sponsor of one of snooker's Triple Crown tournaments - the UK Championship, held at the York Barbican. Since its inception in 1977, some of the sport's biggest players have had their names etched on the coveted trophy, including Steve Davis, Stephen Hendry and Ronnie O'Sullivan.
